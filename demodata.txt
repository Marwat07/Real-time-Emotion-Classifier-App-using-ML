Positive Emotions:

Joy/Happy:

"I am so happy and excited about my vacation next week!"
"This is the best day of my life!"
"I can't stop smiling, I'm just so full of joy."
Surprise:

"Wow, I can't believe I won the lottery!"
"I'm astonished by the beautiful sunset."
"This is an unexpected but wonderful surprise."
Negative Emotions:

Anger:

"I am so angry right now, I can't believe you did that!"
"This is frustrating and unacceptable."
"I'm furious about the terrible service I received."
Sadness:

"I feel so down and lonely today."
"It's a sad day, I miss my friend."
"I'm heartbroken after hearing the news."
Fear:

"I am scared of the dark and what might be hiding in it."
"That loud noise was terrifying."
"I'm afraid of what might happen next."
Disgust:

"That's a disgusting smell."
"I'm repulsed by his behavior."
"The sight of it made me feel sick."
Neutral Emotion:

Neutral:
"The weather is nice today."
"I am going to the store to buy some groceries."
"The meeting is scheduled for 3 PM."
